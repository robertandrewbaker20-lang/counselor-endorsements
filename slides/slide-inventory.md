### Slide 4 — What Is a Deepfake?
- **Definition:** A video, image, or audio clip generated by AI that shows a real person doing or saying something they never did
- **Tech note:** The tech is called generative AI — it learns from millions of examples, then creates new content
- **Timeline:** 5 years ago: required experts and powerful computers · Today: anyone with a phone and $10

### Slide 5 — Why This Matters
- **Title:** Deepfakes In The Real World
- **Examples:** Scam calls ("Grandma, I'm in trouble…") · Fake endorsements (celebrities promoting crypto scams) · Reputation attacks (fake videos shared at school)
